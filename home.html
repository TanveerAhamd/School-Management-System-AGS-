HOW IT WORKS

From Deal to Delivery in 4 Steps

Convert any client agreement into a professional, controlled project workspace.

1

Create Project

Add client details, set price, deadline, and service type. System generates secure client link.

2

Upload & Collaborate

Team uploads drafts internally. Admin shares with client. Clients give visual feedback directly on files.

3

Client Approves

Client clicks approve button. System records approval with timestamp, IP, and version hash.

4

Pay & Download

Payment unlocks final files. Online or manual verification. Client downloads instantly.
